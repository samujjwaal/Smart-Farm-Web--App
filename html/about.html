About Us

Vision

Smart Farming System is being developed to automate certain activities and reduce the human work. This system monitors and keeps track of the soil humidity and temperature using suitable sensors. It then collaborates with the irrigation system using IoT and also stores all the recorded values for prediction for the near future. Big data analytics can be used to make such predictions for future references.

Need for Smart Farm System

In India there is scarcity of water for farming purposes and there is a need of an efficient system to irrigate fields and also save water to a great extent. So this system uses soil humidity and temperature sensors to determine the quantity of water present inside the soil. The user can also see which area of the field requires water at present on a digital mapping of the land. By maintaining a database of water needs of different types and categories of crops it can be determined that how much minimum water is sufficient for a particular crop. Here drip irrigation should be used to deliver water most efficiently.

Future Scope

The data recorded by the humidity and temperature sensors will be stored in an archival system. Using concept of “regression model” and Big Data analytics the data can be plotted and mapped to predict future values of humidity and temperature. Here climate of the place, amount of water consumed by a particular crop and amount of organic substances in the soil will be important factors in prediction and determining the future values of humidity and temperature. This system eliminates the need of the user to analyse the soil and provide water to the farms on his own. The farmer only needs to input correct crop type and geological informations, and rest of the operation shall be handled by the Smart Farming system.
